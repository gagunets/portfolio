SokolFit Mobile Application

SokolFit App - an application for those who do CrossFit every day.

Problem: Regular CrossFit training includes high-intensity workout which consists of a certain amount of elements and movements. This amount and elements itself can vary in an infinite way. Every new training means you need a new workout. Most people who don’t have a coach or a training program have to search for it or come up with it every day.

Purpose: Create an app that will save a bunch of crossfitters time while looking for a needed workout. SokolFit App solves this problem perfectly.

SOLUTION: Created app with an intuitive UX&UI gives you an ability to search and find the needed workout simple and fast. Just a quick filter to choose needed settings (difficulty, modality, type, movement, equipment, or tag). Or search the workout by typing the name, or related words. Random selection is also available.
